2. Upload every file and folder from this corrected package into public_html.
3. Choose overwrite/replace when Hostinger asks about existing files.
4. Confirm these files exist after upload:
   assets/site-v18-1.css
   assets/site-v18-1.js
   assets/messages-v18-1.js
   assets/pdf-v18-1.js
   assets/app-v18-1.js
5. Open app.html and confirm its source references the five files above.
6. Test Download printable PDF, Share card image, Share PDF, PNG and JPEG.
