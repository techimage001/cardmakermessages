Christmas card messages

Christmas card messages can be warm, religious, professional or playful depending on the recipient. A useful structure is a seasonal greeting, one sincere wish and a closing that fits your relationship.

Wedding card messages

Wedding card messages should celebrate the couple without making promises on their behalf. Offer congratulations, name a quality you admire in their relationship and wish them well for the life they are building.

Thank you card messages

Thank you card messages feel sincere when they name what the person did and explain why it mattered. Begin with thanks, mention the specific help, gift or kindness and describe the effect it had.

Congratulations card messages

Congratulations card messages should recognise both the achievement and the effort behind it. State what you are celebrating, mention a quality such as persistence or courage and offer a positive wish for what comes next.

New baby card messages

New baby card messages should welcome the child and support the family without giving unsolicited advice. Congratulate the parents, wish the baby health and love and recognise the joy of the new arrival.

Retirement card messages

Retirement card messages should recognise contribution, character and the new freedom ahead. Thank the person for something specific, mention the difference they made and wish them a fulfilling next chapter.

Get well card messages

Get well card messages should offer care without pressuring the recipient to recover quickly or reply. Say that you are thinking of them, wish them comfort and offer practical support when appropriate.

Valentine’s card messages

Valentine’s card messages feel romantic when they are specific, honest and connected to everyday life. Mention what you value about the person, how the relationship affects you and one hope for the future.

Graduation card messages

Graduation card messages should celebrate the achievement, recognise the effort behind it and encourage the graduate without deciding their future for them. Mention pride, resilience or growth and wish them confidence for the next stage.

Mother’s Day card messages

Mother’s Day card messages feel most meaningful when they name a real form of care, encouragement or sacrifice. Write for the relationship you actually have, whether that is Mum, a grandmother, stepmum or another mother figure.
