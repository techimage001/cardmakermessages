What information should an online invitation include?

A useful invitation clearly states what the event is, who it celebrates, when and where it happens and how guests should respond. Add dress information, age guidance or gift notes only when necessary. The most important details should remain visible at a glance on a phone screen.

Helpful answers

Questions about Invitation Maker

Can I rewrite every part of the invitation maker?

Yes. The invitation maker keeps the final message, front heading, short cover line, recipient, personal detail and signature editable throughout all three stages. Suggested wording is only a starting point.

Which sizes are available in the invitation maker?

The invitation maker includes editable event wording, date, time, venue, host and RSVP details. You can export a social image or a one-page printable PDF after checking the finished layout.

How does the invitation maker stay easy to use?

The invitation maker uses complete one-tap designs instead of layers, handles and free positioning. Automatic spacing, readable contrast and protected margins do the design work while you keep control of the words and colours.
