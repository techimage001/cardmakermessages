How do I choose the right invitation template?

Match the template to the event’s formality, audience and amount of information. A children’s party can use bright playful styling, while a wedding benefits from calm typography and more space. Choose a photo template only when the image supports the event details instead of competing with them.

Questions about Invitation Templates

Can I rewrite every part of the invitation templates?

Yes. The invitation templates keeps the final message, front heading, short cover line, recipient, personal detail and signature editable throughout all three stages. Suggested wording is only a starting point.

Which sizes are available in the invitation templates?

The invitation templates includes editable event wording, date, time, venue, host and RSVP details. You can export a social image or a one-page printable PDF after checking the finished layout.

How does the invitation templates stay easy to use?

The invitation templates uses complete one-tap designs instead of layers, handles and free positioning. Automatic spacing, readable contrast and protected margins do the design work while you keep control of the words and colours.
