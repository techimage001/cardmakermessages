Questions about Digital Card Maker

Can I rewrite every part of the digital card maker?

Yes. The digital card maker keeps the final message, front heading, short cover line, recipient, personal detail and signature editable throughout all three stages. Suggested wording is only a starting point.

Which sizes are available in the digital card maker?

The digital card maker supports social images, flat printable PDFs and folded cards. Choose the format at the final stage, select the exact size and review the finished design before the file opens.

How does the digital card maker stay easy to use?

The digital card maker uses complete one-tap designs instead of layers, handles and free positioning. Automatic spacing, readable contrast and protected margins do the design work while you keep control of the words and colours.
