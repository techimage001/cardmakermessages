Christmas card messages

Christmas card messages can be warm, religious, professional or playful depending on the recipient. A useful structure is a seasonal greeting, one sincere wish and a closing that fits your relationship.

Wedding card messages

Wedding card messages should celebrate the couple without making promises on their behalf. Offer congratulations, name a quality you admire in their relationship and wish them well for the life they are building.

Thank you card messages

Thank you card messages feel sincere when they name what the person did and explain why it mattered. Begin with thanks, mention the specific help, gift or kindness and describe the effect it had.

Congratulations card messages

Congratulations card messages should recognise both the achievement and the effort behind it. State what you are celebrating, mention a quality such as persistence or courage and offer a positive wish for what comes next.
