How do I design a Christmas card that feels polished?

Choose one clear visual direction and keep the message readable. Family photos suit restrained frames, while illustrated cards can use stronger festive colour. Business Christmas cards should remain inclusive and professional. A short cover greeting and a warmer inside message usually create better balance than placing everything on the front.
