CARD MAKER MESSAGES V7

PHOTO POSITIONING
- Uploaded photos appear immediately in a dedicated top photo area.
- Drag directly in the live card with a mouse or finger.
- Zoom, centre, arrow-button and keyboard positioning controls are included.
- The selected crop is stored in the card state and reused for review, PNG, JPG and PDF output.
- Large photos are resized locally in the browser before storage. They are not uploaded to the server.
- Greeting cards, postcards and invitations can use the top photo area.

VERIFIED EMAIL ACCESS
- Uses the layered verification pattern supplied in strongmomquotes-SEO-UPDATEv8.zip.
- Authenticated Hostinger SMTP is used instead of unauthenticated PHP mail.
- Includes honeypot, timing check, browser SHA-256 request token, IP-hash rate limiting, disposable-address blocking and MX/A-domain checking.
- A time-limited private link must be opened before unlimited access is unlocked.
- SMTP password and site salt stay in cmm_private/secrets.php outside public_html and Git.
