How do I make a Mother’s Day card more meaningful?

Mention one specific form of care, encouragement or sacrifice you appreciate. Use the relationship term that feels right and avoid assuming every family story is the same. A photo can add warmth, but a sincere message about an ordinary thing she does often creates the strongest personal connection.
